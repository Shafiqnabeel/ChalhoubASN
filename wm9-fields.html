This visual guide highlights the exact fields required for each tab in the WM9 system to ensure a successful ASN creation.

1. ASN/Receipts Tab

Header Setup

When creating a new record, ensure these header fields are correct:

  • Owner: Enter the correct Owner Code.
  • Document Received Date: Enter the actual date of creation (today's date).

2. General Tab

Shipment Details

Fill in the following details in the General section then click Save:

  • Invoice Number: The official invoice identifier.
  • Date of Invoice: The date as per the actual invoice.
  • Number of Boxes: Use UDF 4 for Cartons or UDF 5 for Pallets. (Fill either one as applicable).
üí° Next Action: Right after saving, you must action -> populate from PO to verify if the quantities and values match your invoice.

3. Carrier Tab

Logistics Traceability

The Carrier tab tracks the reference ID for the shipment:

  • Remarks: Enter the LOG ID being worked on currently.

4. Loading Tab

Transport Definition

The final tab defines the physical transport details:

  • Traffic File Number: Paste the LOG ID.
  • Transportation Mode: Land, Air, or Sea.
  • Terms of Delivery: Respective INCO terms.
  • Country of Destination & Origin: Correct regional codes.
‚úÖ Finalize & Confirm: Once these 4 sections are completed in order, click Save and then Confirm to finalize your WM9 ASN.
