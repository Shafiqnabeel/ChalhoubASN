If a Purchase Order (PO) is not reflecting in the system or you encounter technical issues, follow these steps to report the incident via One Reach.

1. Access One Reach

Begin by logging into the One Reach support portal.

Go to One Reach Support
  • Initiate Report Click on "New" and then select "Report an Issue" from the menu.
  • General Information Fill out the basic details including:
    • Brand: Select your respective brand.
    • Function Location: Should always be Warehouse.
    • Warehouse Location: Select the relevant warehouse.
  • Categorization (Critical) To ensure your ticket reaches the correct team, use these exact settings:
    • Category: Warehouse Management System
    • Sub-Category: ASN / Inbound
    • Note: If this is a Sephora incident, the sub-category must be 3PL Issues.
  • Subject & Description The subject should clearly state the issue.
    Example: PO 123456 is not reflecting in WM9 / SCE11 / ASN Portal

    Copy this same text into the Description section before submitting.

    Pro Tip: Add the BU to the ticket while creating it by clicking the "Add CC" button so they can track progress.

2. Post-Submission Actions

  • Retrieve Incident Number Once submitted, scroll to the top of the page to find your Incident Number (e.g., #INC-1883087).
  • Forgotten CC? If you forgot to add the BU during creation, you can scroll to the top of the submitted incident and click "Add People".
  • Communication Send an email to the respective BU informing them of the issue and include the Incident Number for their reference.
