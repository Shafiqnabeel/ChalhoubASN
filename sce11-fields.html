This visual guide highlights the exact fields required for each tab in the SCE11 system to ensure a successful ASN creation.

1. ASN/Receipt Initialization

Required Header Fields

Once you click "New", fill in these two sections immediately:

  • PO Number: The official Purchase Order number.
  • Documents Received Date: Enter the actual date of creation (today's date).

2. General Tab

Invoice Identification

Navigate to the General Tab to identify the shipment:

  • Supplier Invoice Number: Enter the precise invoice number associated with the goods.

3. Load Tab

Logistics Configuration

Enter the following logistics details to define the transport:

  • Traffic File Number (LOG ID)
  • Transportation Mode
  • Terms of Delivery
  • Country of Origin & Destination

4. User Defined Tab (UDF)

Financial & Physical Data

Complete the record with the following details:

  • Invoice Value & Total ASN Value: Fill as per the invoice document. Note: For Intercompany ASNs, do NOT amend the price.
  • Package Count: Enter the Total Number of Cases or Pallets depending on the shipment.
  • Invoice Date: Select the official date listed on the invoice.
‚úÖ Validation Ready: Once these fields are verified against your documents, proceed to click Save. Once saved, your ASN is successfully created and ready.
