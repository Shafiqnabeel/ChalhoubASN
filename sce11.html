Creating An ASN In SCE11

Use this legacy system for international freight and hard-goods (furniture/fixtures).

  • Intercompany / Inter-DSO Processing For Intercompany or Inter-DSO PO types, follow this logic:
    • Navigate to the respective Warehouse location.
    • Go to the ASN Tab.
    • Paste the PO number between two percent signs (e.g., %123456%) in the External Receipt section.
  • Access the Gateway (Manual Entries) For manual international freight, select "New Transaction" and choose "856 - Ship Notice / Manifest" from the dropdown.
  • Header Information Fill in the Bill of Lading (BOL) number and the Ship-To Location code (e.g., WH-01).
  • Itemization Upload the CSV template with SKU counts. Ensure there are no discrepancies between the PO and the physical shipment.
  • Finalize & Transmit Click "Validate" to check for errors. If green, click "Transmit" to send the data to our Warehouse Management System (WMS).
